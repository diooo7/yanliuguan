Kendo Practice Guidelines

  • Beginners must attend trial classes to learn basic kendo
  • Wear comfortable sportswear for practice, kendo uniform required for formal classes
  • Warm up thoroughly before each practice to prevent injuries
  • Strictly follow safety regulations during practice
  • Recommend attending at least 2 classes per week to maintain progress
